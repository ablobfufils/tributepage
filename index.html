Peyton Manning's Illustrious NFL Career


Peyton Manning was an NFL Quarterback for the Indianapolis Colts and the Denver Broncos. He enjoyed a decorated career and has many accolades to his name.
His Hall of Fame resume includes:

  • 2x Super Bowl Champion
  • 5x NFL MVP (03, 04, 08, 09, 2013)
  • 14x Pro Bowler
  • 2x NFL Offensive Player of the Year
  • 7x First Team All-Pro
